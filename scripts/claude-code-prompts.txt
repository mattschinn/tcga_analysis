Lastly, Notebook 04 focuses on discordant outlier group by applying ML to a high-confidence concordant subgroup that excludes the outliers from training. for detail on the discordant outlier group (whre HER2 label and RNA expression data do not agree) see "discordant-group" markup tagged section below.
